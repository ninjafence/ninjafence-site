About

Hi, I’m Ninja — an embedded software engineer with over 10 years of experience in building real-world systems.

I’ve always loved woodworking. My father is a master carpenter with more than 30 years of experience, and all the woodworking tools I use today come from him. He is still actively working in the workshop.

The ideas behind NinjaFence and NinjaDCC come directly from real problems we face in daily woodworking. I started building these tools to help my father work more efficiently and comfortably.

If these tools can also help other woodworkers, that would mean a lot to me.
